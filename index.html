About ZICA

For seventeen years, Zee Institute of Creative Art (ZICA), India's pioneering Classical and Digital Animation Training Academy, has cultivated exceptional talent in both 2D and 3D animation. ZICA stands as the nation's first comprehensive institution dedicated to this art, empowering young individuals to excel. Their unique training approach centers on fostering an engaging and vibrant learning atmosphere, ensuring students thrive creatively. This commitment has positioned ZICA as a leading force in nurturing the future of animation.

Now spanning over 30 centers across 15+ key Indian cities, from Mumbai to Ahmedabad, ZICA has become the preferred choice for aspiring young professionals seeking to transform their passion into a career. It empowers India's youth, solidifying its position as a leading animation education provider.
