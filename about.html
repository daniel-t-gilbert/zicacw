About ZICA

For seventeen years, Zee Institute of Creative Art (ZICA), India's pioneering Classical and Digital Animation Training Academy, has cultivated exceptional talent in both 2D and 3D animation. ZICA stands as the nation's first comprehensive institution dedicated to this art, empowering young individuals to excel. Their unique training approach centers on fostering an engaging and vibrant learning atmosphere, ensuring students thrive creatively. This commitment has positioned ZICA as a leading force in nurturing the future of animation.

Now spanning over 30 centers across 15+ key Indian cities, from Mumbai to Ahmedabad, ZICA has become the preferred choice for aspiring young professionals seeking to transform their passion into a career. It empowers India's youth, solidifying its position as a leading animation education provider.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Are drawing skills compulsory?

For 2D animation drawing skills is a must. If you are more interested in the 3D animation, then you will suffice without drawing.

2. Which course should I prefer after 12th?

After 12th you should opt for a career-oriented program. The duration of the program should be 2 years or more.

3. What type of courses are available?

ZICA has programs from 2D animation, 3D animation, Visual Effects, Graphic design, Web design, Interior design, Fashion Design etc. The duration of the program varies from 3 months to 3 years. Two-to-three-year programs are career oriented and comprehensive programs for 10th or 12th pass students. One year programs are very focused industry standard programs for the students who are studying or completed Graduation or Post-Graduation.

4. Is the faculty experienced?

Yes. We hire faculties with a minimum of 2 years of production and 2 years of training experience.
