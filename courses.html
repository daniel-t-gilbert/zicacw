BSc Game Designing & Development

BSc Game Designing & Development is a 3-year undergraduate program crafted for aspiring game creators who want to turn their passion into a profession. The course blends art, design, and technology—covering game concept development, 2D & 3D design, level design, programming, UI/UX, and game engines like Unity and Unreal.

BSc Interior Designing

BSc Interior Design is a 3-year undergraduate program designed to nurture creative thinkers and transform them into professional interior designers. The course covers essential aspects of spatial design, including residential, commercial, and public spaces, with a focus on aesthetics, functionality, sustainability, and user experience.
Students will learn design principles, space planning, materials and finishes, lighting, furniture design, CAD software, and 3D visualization tools. Through hands-on projects, industry exposure, and design workshops, they will develop the skills and confidence to create impactful interior spaces that blend beauty with purpose.

Blender

This Blender program covers a comprehensive range of topics essential for 3D modeling and animation. It starts with fundamental techniques such as creating and transforming mesh objects, utilizing modifiers and modifiers for symmetrical objects, and advanced operations like Boolean operations and UV mapping. Rigging is explored for various purposes including character animation and mechanical rigging. Particle systems, dynamics, and simulations are also covered extensively. Additionally, it delves into animation principles, techniques, and tools like keyframing, graph editor, and pose libraries. The program wraps up with advanced features like non-linear animation editing and audio integration, encapsulating a holistic learning journey.
